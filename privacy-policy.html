Policy

Last Updated: June 2026  ·  Mathare North SDA Church, Nairobi, Kenya

1. Who We Are

Mathare North SDA Church ("we", "us", "our") is a Christ-centered Christian community based in Nairobi, Kenya. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect personal information submitted through our website at mnchurch.org.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily submit it through our website forms. This may include:

  • Full name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Prayer requests and personal messages
  • Membership application details (address, transfer type)
  • Child dedication details (child's name, date of birth, parent contact)
  • Pastoral visitation requests (address, reason for visit)
  • Newsletter subscription email address

We do not collect payment card data, government identification numbers, or sensitive financial information through this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your prayer requests and contact messages
  • To process membership transfer or child dedication requests
  • To arrange pastoral visitation as requested
  • To send church event updates and newsletters (where consent is given)
  • To maintain accurate church membership records

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secured MySQL database hosted on our server. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted HTTPS connections, server-side validation, and restricted database access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Email Communications

When you submit a form, an email notification is sent to our church administration at zephoduor14@gmail.com. You may receive a reply from our pastoral team. You can opt out of newsletters at any time by contacting us.

6. Cloudflare Turnstile

Our forms are protected by Cloudflare Turnstile, a privacy-preserving anti-spam solution. Turnstile may collect limited technical data (IP address, browser characteristics) to distinguish humans from automated bots. This data is processed by Cloudflare under their Privacy Policy.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Cloudflare Turnstile may set a functional cookie required for spam protection. No analytics or marketing cookies are used.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at office@mnchurch.org. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Child dedication forms collect information about minors submitted by their parents or guardians. We treat this data with the highest care and use it only for the purposes of the dedication service.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at:
