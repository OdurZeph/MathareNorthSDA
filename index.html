/ OUR STORY

FAITH
IN EVERY
MOMENT

Mathare North SDA Church is a sanctuary where faith meets community. We are dedicated to creating an environment where everyone can experience the transformative power of God's love, find purpose, and build meaningful relationships.

We believe that every life has a divine purpose. That's why we focus on authentic worship, practical teaching, and outreach that makes a difference — from our local neighborhood to the global community.
