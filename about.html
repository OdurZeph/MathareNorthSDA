Who We Are:
Mathare North SDA Church Nairobi

A Christ-centered church in Nairobi built on faith, love, and transformation.

Discover Our Story

/ JOURNEY OF FAITH

Our Story

2005

The Humble Beginning

Mathare North SDA Church started in a small living room with just five families, united by a vision for authentic worship and community impact.

2012

Expanding Our Reach

As our congregation grew, we moved into our first dedicated sanctuary, launching our outreach and youth programs to serve the wider community.

2020

A Global Community

Embracing the digital age, we launched our online campus, connecting thousands of believers worldwide through worship and teaching.

/ CORE DOCTRINE

What We Believe

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is the Son of God, our Lord and Savior, and the only way to the Father.

Salvation

We believe salvation is a free gift from God, received through faith and grace alone.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired and authoritative Word of God, our final guide for life.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day power and gifts of the Holy Spirit to empower believers.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, a family called to worship and serve together.

The Great Commission

We believe in our call to make disciples of all nations, sharing the gospel globally.
